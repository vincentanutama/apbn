Filename convention

YYYY_A1_V1_YYMMDD

Components:

1. YYYY = Tahun Anggaran

2. A/R  = Anggaran/Realisasi

3.
1 Ringkasan
2 Belanja per Fungsi
3 Pendapatan per jenis pajak
4 Pendapatan per jenis retribusi
5 Semester 1
6 Neraca

4. version

5. Date Update

Usually only A/R1 (Ringkasan) has multiple updates
